SCARAB, DIGITAL GAME (2024)

"You shall weigh your heart on the scale. Should it prove lighter than a feather, you shall proceed. Should it weigh heavier, you shall not."

Exiled and on the brink of death, Nomad awakens in the Egyptian-inspired Afterlife. With the sun vanquished and magic fading, they must navigate a perilous purgatory, confront twisted guardians, and reach the gates of DUAT to restore light, reclaim lost magic, and fight for a way home.

Game Production & Project Lead, Demi Saleeb. Animation by Helen Juwon Park. 3D Models by Demi Saleeb. Character Design by Jeffrey Huang. Concept Art (original character, prop and environmental design) by Demi Saleeb. Environmental Textures by Helen Juwon Park & Julianna Ernie. Level Design by Yongija Tang. Music/SFX by Sean Son & Aderyn Clark. Programming by DanielKao & Woyu Wang. UI/UX by Michelle Chen. Voice Acting by Akemi Webster, Demi Saleeb, Ifeanyichukwu Onyeanakwe, Karen Truong, & Sock Pan Rah. Writing by Akemi Webster, Anthony Nash, & Demi Saleeb.
