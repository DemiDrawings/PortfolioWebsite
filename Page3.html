Overview

A purgatory built
from myth and shadow.

Exiled and on the brink of death, Nomad awakens in the Egyptian-inspired Afterlife.

With the sun vanquished and magic fading, they must navigate a perilous purgatory, confront twisted guardians, and reach the gates of DUAT to restore light, reclaim lost magic, and fight for a way home.
