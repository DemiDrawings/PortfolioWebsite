FISHY (2025)

All 2D Art, Illustrations, & Animations by Demi Saleeb; 3D Modeling & Programming (C#) by Vinny.

Created within the Design Media Arts (DMA) Collaborative Game Class at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA), "Fishy" is a one week, collaborative game jam inspired by the prompt: fishing. Explore a completely normal, non-existential, fish feeding simulator!

“Fishy” explores consumerism; working as a clog within the corporate machine, helpless and subservient to the unending cycle of feeding and exploitation. For instance, if Fishy is not fed (whether as a means of opposition to this cruelty and food production at large, or simple idleness), it will die. Contrary, if you continue to feed Fishy, it will meet the same fate in being sold for consumption. Although it was intended to have different endings—such as helping Fishy escape, though getting fired from your job in the process—the time constraint made us choose to feed( see what I did there?) into the game’s narrative of successive consumerism.
