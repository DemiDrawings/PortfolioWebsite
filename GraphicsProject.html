UCLA Athletics Graphics

Dynamic Social Media Campaign

Created dynamic graphics for the Athletics Department at the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). This work was featured across the university's official social media channels, reaching thousands of students, alumni, and sports enthusiasts.

The design approach focused on capturing the energy and spirit of UCLA's athletic programs while maintaining brand consistency across all platforms. Each graphic was crafted to maximize engagement and celebrate the achievements of student athletes.

Design Process

From Concept to Execution

The design process began with understanding the athletic department's brand guidelines and social media strategy. Each graphic needed to work seamlessly across multiple platforms while maintaining visual impact and readability.

Working closely with the athletics team, I developed templates that could be quickly adapted for different sports and events while maintaining a cohesive visual identity.
