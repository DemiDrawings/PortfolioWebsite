Poetry

Transcendence

2022 — Lyric

I transcend setback into opportunity: I can turn a blank canvas into color chart my mind with ink and lead pick up broken fragments— honeyed with molten gold where once was cleft That is the work of ruined hands that is what it means to be a creator, to be past the breaking point on the verge of decay and casting light Everything the world sought to unmake.

Broken Constellation

2023 — Lyric

Do you remember the stars and the city the light of a thousand lives we had yet to meet? Through the myriads of people we have become something worse than strangers. Still, I will sit with you. Even in silence.

Devil's Pact

2024 — Lyric

Damnation With hair made up of fire Eyes that court all of fall Glowing like embers in the dark I would never tell How their deviled grin Was a pact of brimstone I, their unwavering clientele Once whispered Like a prayer between teeth Scriptures tried and sold Fallen Angel, disgraced and forgone There is no saint nor devil Just flesh and vessel, who, like the Gods that bane existence, forgets my face in every ordainment.

Hiraeth

2025 — Lyric

When my bones are too weary and there is no one to carry I will think of them fondly when no one is around me Laughing and aching their love recalling like stars you cannot see in the morning
